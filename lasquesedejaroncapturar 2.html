Photography

Las que se dejaron capturar

A long-term collection of portraits centred on gesture, presence and intimacy.

This ongoing series brings together portraits made in different contexts and over several years. Rather than pursuing a single formal style, it focuses on the moment a person relaxes in front of the camera and allows a gesture, look or posture to reveal something unplanned.

Role

Photography, direction and image editing.

Tools

Digital and analogue photography, Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop.

Result

A personal portrait archive that explores trust, spontaneity and the relationship between photographer and subject.
