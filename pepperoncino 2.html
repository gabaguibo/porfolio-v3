PEPPERONCINO

A playful kinetic-typography experiment built around sound and misheard lyrics.

PEPPERONCINO turns the rhythm and phonetics of a song into a humorous typographic performance. The animation uses timing, scale and repetition to make imagined words feel inseparable from the music, treating typography as both image and choreography.

Role

Concept, typography, motion design and editing.

Tools

Adobe After Effects, Adobe Illustrator and Premiere Pro.

Result

A short audiovisual piece that explores rhythm, language and visual humour through kinetic type.
